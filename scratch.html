Why Training and Recovery Matter

Football performance is not just about lifting heavy or practicing hard. Athletes also need proper sleep, nutrition, hydration, mobility, and recovery time so their bodies can adapt and improve.

Key Parts of Athletic Development

  1. Training
    • Strength work
    • Conditioning
    • Position-specific drills
  2. Recovery
    • Sleep
    • Nutrition
    • Hydration
  3. Performance
    • Explosiveness
    • Endurance
    • Consistency
